The Dark Age









This game inspired from the world of darkness and ugliness, the story begins with a normal highschool student (player's role) travels back to the Dark age world by an accident. To accomplish the game, players have to travel and explore the whole world, find out the way to get back to the real world and continue the normal life of a student.
